Selected Projects

Wellbeing and Prosociality in Driving - Honda Research Institute

Designed a longitudinal study on mindful interventions and prosociality, employing NLP for sentiment and content analysis, and collecting data via a VR-based simulator.

Aggressive Driving & Road Rage — AAA Foundation for Traffic Safety

Analyzed data from a nationally representative sample of American drivers to understand the perceptions and understanding of road rage, in addition to the relationships between aggressive driving behaviors.

Response to Automation Uncertianty — Toyota CSRC

Designed a simulator study to observe driver engagement in automation, response to system failures, and changes when engegd in a secondary task.
